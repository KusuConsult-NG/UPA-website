Welcome to the **UPA Website Content Template**! This document is designed to guide you (the client) in providing the official text, specifications, and contact details for the new UPA marketing website.

---

## 🎨 Brand Tone & Writing Guidelines

*   **Warm & Encouraging**: The language should feel like a supportive partner helping people improve their lifestyles.
*   **Accessible & Simple**: Avoid heavy financial or technical jargon. Keep processes simple to understand.
*   **Empathetic & HOPE-Filled**: Strictly avoid aggressive collection-oriented language, debt-related warnings, or enforcement-heavy corporate wording. 
*   *Yes:* *"We know life comes with unexpected responsibilities. Our customer care team is here to help you adjust your plan smoothly."*
*   *No:* *"Late payments will result in penalties, credit blocks, and immediate collection enforcement."*
